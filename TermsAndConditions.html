Submission Process

TurnShop Corp. will consider the following Manuscript submission processes for the following book genres.

Submission Genre

  • General Fiction
  • Drama
  • Collection of Short Stories
  • Science Fiction
  • Historical Fiction
  • Poetry
  • Anthologies
  • Fantasy
  • Plays

Submission Format

  • Manuscripts must be typed using Times New Roman with a 12 point font size, 1.5 spacing.
  • We do accept handwritten nor hard copy manuscript submissions.
  • Please make sure that manuscripts do not have the printing functionality disabled.
  • If watermarks are included, please ensure that the text behind the watermark is legible.
    Most importantly, files MUST be sent in PDF or Microsoft Word formats (we will not accept files saved in WordPad or sent via links to a website).
  • Please do not paste or type your submission in the body of the email.
  • The first three chapters or the first fifty pages with a thorough synopsis should please be submitted.
  • The subject of your email should be the title of your manuscript.
  • The body of your email should please be polite.

Emails should be sent to TurnShopCorp@gmail.com
