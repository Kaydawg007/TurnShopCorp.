OluKorede Yishau Biography

Olukorede S. Yishau, the Nigeria Prize for Literature nominee, award-winning journalist, novelist, and short-story writer, was born in Lagos, Nigeria. His first novel, In the Name of Our Father, has been a subject of theses by students in Nigerian universities. It was described by two-time Booker finalist Chigozie Obioma as a 'work of deceptive simplicity'. His collection of short stories Vaults of Secrets has received rave advance reviews.
