Who We Are

TurnShop Corp is a leading pushlishing outlet in America. Our company was birth by famous US based Writer Kayode Adams. Having been publishing for students, IT Professional and researchers since 1985, we have been able to amass a great list of clientelle. The company came to spolight when we published "Harry Poter" by J.K Rowling in 1997. Which we continued to publish all through to 2007. Since then, we have went on to publish for more famous names. Amongst them being "Long Walk To Freedom" by Nelson Mandela, "Things Fall Apart" by Chinua Achebe, "Gifted Hands" Ben Carson's and lots more! Our company is the current publisher for Astronomical researcher Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson. We also have the highly respected tech guru, Mosh Hamedani, on our client list. Other clients include Pulitzer winners Clark Kent and Lois Lane . We do not only publish for Americans, we published "In the name of Our Father" by OluKorede Yishau. We boast of over 20 retail outlets in America and have branched out to Africa in recent years, with four retails outlets, one in Nigeria, two in South Africa and one in Ghana. After over 35 years of publishing, we definitely aren't stopping now!

Our Mission

The TurnShop Corp. aimed to be the leading publishing house in America and they, after over 30 years of being at the top of the Publishing chain in America. Our aim is to spread across the world which we have kickstarted with our Africa operations initiative having constructed four retail outlets in Nigeria, South Africa and Ghana. We intend to branch out to China, Dubai and France and we currently have a retail outlets in construction in all three countries.
